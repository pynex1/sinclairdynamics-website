Practical Applications

The most useful AI and digital work is rarely about the technology itself — it is about finding the places where the right tool removes the right friction and allows people to focus on what actually matters.

AI-Assisted Document Production. Business plans, proposals, executive summaries, technical reports, tender documents, and project correspondence — produced faster and at higher quality through AI collaboration, with full professional review and accountability for every output.

Engineering Design Exploration. AI as a collaborative thinking partner for working through design constraints, evaluating trade-offs, and exploring optimisation options before committing resources to a particular direction.

Process & Workflow Automation. Identifying and eliminating the manual, repetitive steps that slow teams down — using automation tools that connect systems, reduce friction, and let people focus on higher-value work.

Building & Home Automation. Implementing connected building management systems for residential and light commercial environments — integrating energy systems, appliances, security, and environmental controls into unified, intelligently managed platforms. I have built and operate my own whole-home automation system with Modbus integration to a Victron inverter, giving me practical, tested experience with real-world implementation challenges.

Digital Tooling Advisory. Helping organisations choose and implement the right digital tools for their specific context — project management platforms, CRM systems, collaboration tools, reporting solutions, and AI applications. Honest, practical advice grounded in actual usage experience.
